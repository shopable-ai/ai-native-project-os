# 风险与发布审核规则

## review.risk.sensitive_data

```yaml
rule_id: review.risk.sensitive_data
version: 1
priority: 10
canonical_path: governance/rules/风险与发布审核规则.md#review.risk.sensitive_data
severity: blocking
applies_to:
  data_classes: [project_declared]
required_evidence: [data_classification, disclosure_policy_ref, subject_content]
allowed_outcomes: [allow, rewrite_required, blocked, rule_gap]
failure_action: block_and_preserve_audit_evidence
```

审核对象必须满足项目声明的数据分类、最小披露和秘密处理规则。疑似泄漏或规则范围不足时立即阻断并保存审计 Evidence；不得用改写结果覆盖原始失败记录。

## review.release.commitment_boundary

```yaml
rule_id: review.release.commitment_boundary
version: 1
priority: 30
canonical_path: governance/rules/风险与发布审核规则.md#review.release.commitment_boundary
severity: blocking
applies_to:
  artifact_classes: [generated_content, release_candidate]
required_evidence: [project_fact_refs, authorization_boundary_refs, subject_content]
allowed_outcomes: [allow, rewrite_required, blocked, rule_gap]
failure_action: rewrite_or_block
```

审核对能力、价格、时效、承诺和发布条件的表达是否超出批准事实或授权边界。内容通过只允许进入后续 gate，不产生真实发送、付款、删除或生产发布权限。
